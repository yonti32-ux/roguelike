# Game Improvements & Expansion Suggestions

## üéØ Core Gameplay Enhancements

### 1. **Endgame & Progression Goals**
- **Boss Floors**: Every 5-10 floors, introduce unique boss encounters with special mechanics
- **Final Boss**: Add a climactic final boss at floor 20/25/50 (configurable depth)
- **Ascension System**: After beating the game, allow "ascending" with permanent meta-progress
- **Achievement System**: Track accomplishments (floors cleared, enemies defeated, perfect runs)
- **Daily Challenges**: Rotating daily/weekly challenges with special modifiers

### 2. **Deeper Progression Systems**
- **Prestige/Meta-Progression**: Unlock permanent bonuses after completing runs
  - Starting gold bonuses
  - Unlock new starting items
  - Unlock new companion options
  - Unlock new classes
- **Character Relationships**: Companions could have relationship meters that unlock special abilities
- **Reputation System**: Track player actions that affect shop prices, event outcomes
- **Mastery System**: Gain mastery points for using skills, unlock skill variants

### 3. **More Content Variety**

#### **Classes**
- Add 2-3 more classes:
  - **Paladin**: Hybrid tank/healer
  - **Ranger**: Ranged DPS with pet mechanics
  - **Necromancer**: Summoner/DoT specialist
  - **Monk**: Unarmed fighter with combo system

#### **Enemies**
- **Elite Variants**: Stronger versions of regular enemies with special abilities
- **Minibosses**: Powerful enemies that spawn on specific floors
- **Environmental Enemies**: Traps, animated objects, environmental hazards
- **Boss Mechanics**: Multi-phase bosses, adds spawning, environmental interactions

#### **Items**
- **Set Items**: Equipment sets that grant bonuses when wearing multiple pieces
- **Unique Items**: Legendary items with unique mechanics
- **Item Enchanting**: Allow players to enhance existing items
- **Consumables**: Potions, scrolls, bombs (battle items)
- **Runes/Gems**: Socketable items that add properties to equipment

## üéÆ Gameplay Mechanics

### 4. **Combat System Expansions**

#### **Battle Mechanics**
- **Status Effects Expansion**:
  - Bleed (DoT)
  - Burn (fire DoT)
  - Freeze (stun variant)
  - Poison (stacking DoT)
  - Mark/Expose (increased damage taken)
  - Shield/Bubble (damage absorption)
  
- **Positioning Enhancements**:
  - Backstab bonuses (flanking from behind)
  - Push/pull mechanics
  - Knockback skills
  - Area denial (creating difficult terrain)
  
- **Combo System**: Chain skills together for bonus effects
- **Ultimate Abilities**: Powerful skills with long cooldowns (unlockable)
- **Environmental Interactions**: 
  - Push enemies into hazards
  - Break terrain to create paths
  - Trigger traps strategically

#### **AI Improvements**
- **Smarter Enemy AI**: 
  - Group tactics (focus fire, positioning)
  - Adaptive difficulty
  - Personality-based AI (aggressive, defensive, tactical)
- **Companion AI Options**: Let players set companion AI behavior (aggressive, defensive, support)

### 5. **Exploration Enhancements**

#### **Map Features**
- **Secret Rooms**: Hidden areas with special rewards
- **Biome Variants**: Different dungeon themes (crypt, forest, volcanic, ice caves)
- **Dungeon Modifiers**: Random floor modifiers (enemies deal more damage, more loot, etc.)
- **Multiple Paths**: Branching floor layouts where players choose routes
- **Time-Limited Events**: Limited-time challenges during exploration

#### **Interactions**
- **Traps & Disarming**: Trap detection and disarming minigame/skill check
- **Puzzles**: Simple puzzle rooms for rewards
- **NPC Dialogue**: Expand merchants with dialogue, quests, rumors
- **Faction System**: Multiple factions with different shops/events

### 6. **Event System Expansion**

#### **New Events**
- **Mysterious Stranger**: Offers trades or challenges
- **Training Grounds**: Temporary stat boosts or skill training
- **Trial Rooms**: Combat challenges with specific rules
- **Altar of Sacrifice**: Trade resources for permanent bonuses
- **Memory Shard**: View lore/story snippets
- **Companion Recruitment**: Find and recruit new companions
- **Cursed Shrine**: Risk/reward events with powerful effects

#### **Event Variants**
- Make events more interactive with choices
- Branching outcomes based on player stats/class
- Class-specific event options

## üèÜ Progression & Customization

### 7. **Skill System Enhancements**
- **Skill Synergies**: Skills that combo with each other
- **Skill Modifiers**: Allow customization of skill behavior (e.g., choose between AoE or single-target variants)
- **Passive Skills**: Skills that are always active (not hotbar slots)
- **Skill Trees**: More branching paths in skill progression
- **Skill Fusions**: Combine skills to create hybrid abilities

### 8. **Perk System Expansions**
- **More Perk Branches**: 
  - Alchemy (potions)
  - Leadership (companion bonuses)
  - Survival (exploration benefits)
  - Wealth (gold/loot)
  
- **Perk Synergies**: Perks that work better together
- **Transcendent Perks**: Ultra-rare endgame perks
- **Perk Respec**: Option to respec perks (costs gold or special item)

### 9. **Party System Improvements**
- **Larger Party Size**: Allow 3-4 companions (with balance adjustments)
- **Companion Specialization**: Deepen companion roles (tank, DPS, healer, support)
- **Companion Equipment Slots**: Expand companion equipment (currently only 3 slots)
- **Companion Perks**: Companions get their own perk selections
- **Companion Quests**: Side stories for individual companions
- **Formation System**: Battle formations that provide bonuses

## üîß Technical & Quality of Life

### 13. **Quality of Life Features**
- **Auto-Pickup**: Option to auto-pickup items
- **Auto-Sort Inventory**: Button to organize inventory
- **Equipment Sets**: Save/load equipment loadouts
- **Combat Speed**: Option to speed up combat animations
- **Fast Travel**: Unlock teleporters between floors after clearing them
- **Save Anywhere**: Allow saving during exploration (not just in menus)
- **Undo Last Move**: In battle, allow undoing the last action (limited uses)

### 14. **Balancing & Difficulty**
- **Difficulty Modes**: 
  - Easy (more healing, weaker enemies)
  - Normal (current)
  - Hard (less resources, stronger enemies)
  - Nightmare (extreme challenge)
  
- **Adaptive Difficulty**: Adjust based on player performance
- **Challenge Modifiers**: Let players add difficulty modifiers for bonus rewards
- **Balance Tweaks**: 
  - More granular stat scaling
  - Class rebalancing based on play data
  - Item power curve adjustments

### 15. **Modding & Extensibility**
- **JSON Data**: Move more game data to JSON for easier tweaking
- **Mod Support**: 
  - Custom classes
  - Custom skills
  - Custom items
  - Custom events
- **Editor Tools**: Simple tools for creating content
- **Export/Import**: Share builds/characters
